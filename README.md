Tena360 AI – Wellness Companion for Every Ethiopian
"Affordable Wellness, Personalized for Every Ethiopian"
Overview

Tena360 AI is an AI-powered wellness platform built specifically for Ethiopia. The platform helps individuals achieve healthier lifestyles through personalized nutrition planning, budget-aware meal recommendations, mental wellness support, food recognition technology, farmer-to-consumer marketplace integration, and traditional Ethiopian wellness knowledge.

Unlike many wellness applications designed for high-income markets, Tena360 AI focuses on providing practical and affordable wellness solutions for everyday Ethiopians, including students, families, rural communities, and low-income households.
